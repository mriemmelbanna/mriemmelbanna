# 💫 About Me:
I'm an MIS graduate who spends most of my time on the seam between business problems and technical execution — figuring out what a system needs to do, then actually building it. My main areas are system analysis, full-stack development, and UI/UX.<br><br>I've built my skills through a mix of coursework, the ALX AI Career Essentials program, and the CIB technical bootcamp. I work primarily in Python and C++, build backends with Django, and use PostgreSQL for databases. I also leverage AI Prompt Engineering to streamline development workflows and accelerate problem-solving.<br><br>My graduation project, BloodLink, was a full-stack platform that connects blood donors with hospitals and patients.
